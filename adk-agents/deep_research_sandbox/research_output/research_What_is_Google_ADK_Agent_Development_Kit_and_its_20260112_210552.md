## Google ADK (Agent Development Kit) in 2025

### Key Findings
- Google ADK (Agent Development Kit) is an open-source, flexible, and modular framework for developing and deploying AI agents.
- ADK was introduced at Google Cloud NEXT 2025.
- It is designed to make agent development more akin to traditional software development, providing a code-first programming model.
- The framework is optimized for Gemini models and the Google ecosystem, yet it maintains model-agnostic and deployment-agnostic characteristics, allowing compatibility with other frameworks and models.
- ADK supports the creation and orchestration of multi-agent systems, enabling specialized agents to collaborate on complex tasks.
- It is integrated with Vertex AI Agent Engine Runtime for managed deployment, scaling, security, and monitoring of agents in production.
- A related offering, Agent Designer, provides a no-code/low-code platform within Gemini Enterprise for creating, managing, and launching single and multi-step agents.
- Google Antigravity is an agentic development platform that functions as an IDE, providing an agent-first interface for autonomous task planning, execution, and verification across editor, terminal, and browser environments.
